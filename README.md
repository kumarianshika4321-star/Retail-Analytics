📊 Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard designed to analyze retail business performance across sales, profit, customers, products, regions, and discount strategies. This project focuses on converting raw retail data into meaningful, actionable insights using data visualization and business intelligence techniques.

🚀 Project Overview

This dashboard helps answer important business questions such as:

How do sales and profits perform over time?

Which regions and categories generate the highest profit?

What is the impact of discounts on profitability?

Which products are profit makers and which result in losses?

How do customer segments contribute to overall sales?

The goal of this project is to support data-driven decision-making through clear and interactive visuals.

📈 Key Insights

Technology category shows the strongest overall performance

West region contributes the highest profit

High-value customers drive a major share of revenue

Moderate discounts balance sales growth and profitability

A small number of products generate the majority of profit

📂 Dataset Information

The project uses a retail sales dataset containing:

Orders and sales transactions

Customer and segment details

Product categories and sub-categories

Regional and discount information

The dataset is used strictly for learning and analytical purposes.
